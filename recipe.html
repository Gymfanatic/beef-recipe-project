South African Bobotie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 slices of bread, soaked in milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp apricot jam
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add ground meat and curry powder, and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in apricot jam, soaked bread (squeeze out excess milk), raisins, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish.
  6. Beat eggs and milk together, then pour over the meat mixture.
  7. Bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the topping is set and golden brown.

Enjoy your South African Bobotie!
